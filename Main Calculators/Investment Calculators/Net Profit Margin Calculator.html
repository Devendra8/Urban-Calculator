Net Profit Margin Calculator

What is Net profit margin?

The net profit margin, or simply net margin, measures how much net income or profit is generated as a percentage of revenue. It is the ratio of net profits to revenues for a company or business segment.

Net profit margin formula

It is calculated as :

Net Profit margin = Net Profit ⁄ Total revenue x 100

How to Calculate Net profit margin

Suppose, a company A has net profit of $25 and revenue of $100.

Then the netprofit margin will be :

Net Profit Margin = Net Profit/Revenue = ($25/$100)*100 = 25%

Usage of Net profit margin value

• The net profit margin ratio is used to describe a company’s ability to produce profit and to consider several scenarios, such as an increase in expenses which is deemed ineffective. It is used extensively in financial modeling and company valuation.

• A high net profit margin means that a company is able to effectively control its costs and/or provide goods or services at a price significantly higher than its costs.
