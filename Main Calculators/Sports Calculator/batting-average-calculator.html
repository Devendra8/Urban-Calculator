What is Batting Average?

The batting average is the standard measure that has been used to compare batters ever since the early years of professional baseball.

What is a good batting average?

In modern times, a season batting average of . 300 or higher is considered to be excellent, and an average higher than . 400 a nearly unachievable goal.

How do you calculate an Batting Average?

Batting Average = Total number of hits / Total number of at bats
