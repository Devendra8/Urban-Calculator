What is Cricket Batting Average?

Batting Average is the ratio of a player’s number of runs to the number of times they have been out. It is considered a good metric for a player’s skill as a batsman.

What is a good Cricket batting average?

On average, a batting average of 43 – 45 can be considered a good batting average.

How do you calculate an Batting Average?

Batting Average in Cricket = Runs Scored / Times out

Example of Batting Average?

A batter scores at bat 522 times and is out 27 times in that time.
522 ÷ 27 = 19.33.
Therefore, the player’s batting average is 19.33
