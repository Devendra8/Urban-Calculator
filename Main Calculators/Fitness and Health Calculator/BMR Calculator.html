BMR Calculator

Basic Metabolic Rate is the total amount of calories body requires to maintain when it is in rest position.
The Basal Metabolic Rate(BMR) Calculator can be used to calculate the amount of energy expended while at rest in a neutrally temperate environment, and in a post-absorptive state.
